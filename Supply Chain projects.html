Projects in Supply Chain Management

Power BI reports for a global Pharmaceutical company
Helped the client monitor the timeliness and completeness of product deliveries at a global scale, working with their Customer Analytics CoE, to implement OTIF related BI reports.

  • Redesigned the On-Time & Fill-Rate report calculations to accommodate different delivery dates, enhancing accuracy and flexibility in performance tracking.
  • Defined development specifications for Power BI and provided team guidance on DAX best practices, ensuring high-quality and efficient reporting as per client requirements.
  • Led the refactoring of Power BI and Snowflake models to address challenges posed by increased data volume, resulting in a 2.25x improvement in performance and a 2.15x reduction in capacity costs.

Power BI reports for a global Shipping Company
Matt helped the company manage the ship spares supply chain of various shipping companies globally, by designing & developing a set of Power BI reports with advanced UI features and row-level security to provide personalized information on shipments, costs, and ESG metrics. These tools enabled the client to convert raw data into insights, KPIs & market trends and had a profound impact on their business; as they allowed them to operate with emphasis on strategic adjustments and aim to higher efficiency, sustainability and cost savings.

Power BI reporting pack for a Greek manufacturing company
Implemented a set of Power BI reports to help the client monitor their global sales, shipments & production, and manage their safety stock levels, leveraging SAP BW & Proteus WMS data and incorporating Aimplan, for the users to provide detailed input and create custom scenarions for lead times and market demand.

  • Designed a robust dimensional data model to support scalable, high-performance analytics tailored to supply chain and inventory planning use cases.
  • Created reporting tables and interactive Power BI visuals to provide historic insights & future trends on sales, production, and inventory KPIs.
  • Integrated Aimplan visuals within the reports, enabling user input through Power BI tables and custom extensions, which fed directly into safety stock and reorder point calculations.
