Who we are

Matt, the managing partner of Kedros Analytics (Protopapas Analytics LP), has collaborated with global corporations, leading consultancies, and major universities, in various analytical roles, including Business Intelligence Manager, Data Engineer, and Analytics Consultant. He's been providing value by developing automated reports with advanced analytics, building the data warehousing infrastructure, and enabling the use of AI & analytical tools. He has worked with stakeholders to ensure the solutions drive impactful decisions and has guided diverse analytical teams, drawing on his extensive Analytics experience, his Economics & Operational Research knowledge and his expertise on the Microsoft analytical stack.

Matt works as a consultant, freelancer, and with a team of external collaborators, to drive business value for both SMEs and larger corporations.

Matt Protopapas, PhD

Matt is the managing partner at Kedros Analytics. He is a Data & Analytics Consultant with 15+ years of experience (most of them in Microsoft BI - including Fabric), a PhD in Decision Sciences and 10+ Microsoft certifications. He has worked with leading Greek & UK banks, with global consultancies & IT providers, and with corporate clients in sectors such as shipping, pharma and energy.

Eleni Roussi, PhD

Eleni is a limited partner at Kedros Analytics. She holds a PhD in Materials Science from the National Technical University of Athens, and has two decades of scientific research experience. She leads the Beauty & Vintage branch of the company, which focuses in cosmetics.

Labrini Karahountri, MSc

Labrini is an external collaborator at Kedros Analytics. She has 20+ years of experience (seven of which in BI - including Fabric), holds a Master in Big Data and Informatics, and has 10+ Databricks & Microsoft certifications. She has worked with corporate clients in sectors such as supply chain, insurance & professional certifications.

Grigoris Nitas, MEng

Grigoris is an external collaborator of Kedros Analytics. He is a Data & Business Analytics Consultant with 5+ years of experience, a Mechanical & Aeronautics Engineer, and a Microsoft Power BI expert. He has worked with global consulting firms, and in industries such as maritime, energy, waste management, construction, and government.

Tony Loci, MPM

Tony is an external advisor of Kedros Analytics. He has led onsite and remote international teams in IT projects across the FS, AI, Telco, Healthcare, Real Estate & Gaming industries, managing product development and ensuring strategic coordination and successful delivery for over 15 years. He holds a Master in Project Management and is Lean & Agile certified.

Mike Nikolaidis, BSc

Mike is an external collaborator of Kedros Analytics. He is experienced in Sales and Data Analytics, and has a strong academic background in Statistics and Actuarial Mathematics. He has worked with Coca-Cola, as well as companies in the financial and hospitality sectors.
