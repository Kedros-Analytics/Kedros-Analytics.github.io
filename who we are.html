Company & Collaborators

Matt, the managing partner of Kedros Analytics (Protopapas Analytics LP), is a Data & Analytics Consultant with 15+ years of experience in the industry (most of them in Microsoft BI - including Fabric), a PhD in Decision Sciences (and further research experience) and 10+ Microsoft certifications. He has worked with leading Greek & UK banks, with global consultancies & IT providers, and with corporate clients in sectors such as shipping, pharma and energy. He delivers scalable, automated analytics & reporting apps to avance business decision making to the next level.


Matt primarily works as a contractor on long-term analytical projects for major companies, while also offering freelance consulting services and coordinating the efforts of external collaborators and business partners to deliver value in analytics initiatives.
