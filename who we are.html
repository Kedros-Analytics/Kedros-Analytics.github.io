Who we are

Kedros Analytics (Protopapas Analytics LP) is led by Matt Protopapas, Ph.D. who has collaborated with European banks, global consultancies, and major corporations in a variety of analytical roles, including Business Intelligence Manager, Data Engineer and Analytics Consultant, in the last 15 years. He's been providing value by developing automated reports with advanced analytics, building the data warehousing infrastructure, and enabling the use of AI & analytical tools. He has worked with stakeholders to ensure the solutions drive impactful decisions and has guided analytical teams, drawing on his extensive Analytics experience, his Economics & Management knowledge and his expertise on the Microsoft analytical stack.

Matt Protopapas, PhD

Matt is the managing partner at Kedros Analytics. He is a Data & Analytics Consultant with 20+ years of experience, a PhD in Decision Sciences and 10+ Microsoft certifications. He has worked with leading Greek & UK banks, with global consultancies & IT providers, and with corporate clients in sectors such as shipping, pharma and energy.

Eleni Roussi, PhD

Eleni is a limited partner at Kedros Analytics. She holds a PhD in Materials Science from the National Technical University of Athens, and has two decades of scientific research experience. She specializes in the synthesis & characterization of advanced ceramic nano-materials and their applications.
