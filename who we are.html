Who we are

Matt Protopapas enables companies make data driven decisions through BI reports, advanced analytics, and data [ware|lake]houses. He has 20+ years of relevant experience, a PhD in Decision Sciences and 10+ Microsoft certifications.

In his 20+ years' journey, he's collaborated with European banks, global consultancies, and major corporations in a variety of analytical roles, providing value through BI reports implementation & BI models (re)design, robust Data Warehouse development, and AI amp; advanced analytics enablement.

Over the last couple of years, he's been developing Power BI reports with embedded AI & app like features, to help companies make data-driven decisions, while simultaneously building the necessary infrastructure, to ensure they are populated with accurate & up-to-date information.

He's also advised organizations on how to evolve & derive value from their analytical capabilities, and helped guide development teams through his expertise in the Microsoft analytical stack and his experience with Agile.

Matt operates through Kedros Analytics, a company he co-founded with his wife. An accountant friend once advised him that it would be more advantageous to work through his own company rather than as an employee. Taking this advice to heart, he transitioned to contracting through a personal company after a few months. This change allowed him to rediscover his passion for the consultant lifestyle, providing opportunities to tackle diverse and engaging challenges and facilitating seamless involvement in projects beyond Greece.
