What are Self-Service Analytics & BI:

Self-service Analytics, is a user-driven approach to data analysis that empowers non-technical users to access, explore, and visualize data without relying on data specialists. Self-service capabilities typically feature drag-and-drop functionality, pre-built calculations, and AI capabilities, making it easy for business professionals to analyze data in real-time.

What are Advanced Analytics:

Advanced analytics refer to the application of sophisticated analytical techniques derived from Statistics, Econometrics, Operational Research, and Artificial Intelligence, to extract deeper insights from data and support complex business decisions. Unlike traditional Business Intelligence, which focuses on descriptive and potentially diagnostic insights, advanced analytics leverage predictive and prescriptive models to help identify key drivers & understand their impact, control & improve processes, and recommend (or automatically implement) near-optimal actions.

A simple yet practical example of advanced analytics in action is the calculation of safety stocks, reorder points, and minimum order quantities for inventory management.   • Setup proactive alerts when stock nears reorder points, introduce automations & AI agents, to transform insights into actions that generate value.
  • Design and implement further analytics & KPIs for tactical decision-making, to drive longer term, sustainable growth.
