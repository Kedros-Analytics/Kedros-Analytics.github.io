Projects in Academia

Computational Optimization Methods in Statistics, Econometrics and Finance
COMISEF was a Research and Training Network of 11 universities and a leading German bank. I was a Marie Curie Fellow based in University of Rome "La Sapienza". I performed research on time series analysis using genetic algorithms, published several research articles, and delivered trainings & presentations in workshops and conferences. COMISEF contributed to the development of novel methods to derive insight from econometric data, based on computational modeling, simulation & optimization.

European Ramp Metering Project
EURAMP was a collective European action focused on ramp metering control measures in European motorways in the aim of improving safety and increasing efficiency of traffic flow. I was a research associate based in the Dynamic Systems and Simulation Laboratory of the Technical University of Crete, one of project member universities. I performed traffic control data analysis in Matlab and SQL. Major innovation areas and end products of EURAMP included advanced network-wide approaches (including an interoperable software prototype) as well as various safety-critical issues and related implications of ramp metering.

Signal MAnagement in Real Time for urban traffic NETworkS
The aim of the SMART NETS project was to improve the international state-of-the-art in real-time network-wide urban traffic control via the application, demonstration, and evaluation of the new-generation control strategy TUC (Traffic-responsive Urban Control). I was a research associate based in the Dynamic Systems and Simulation Laboratory of the Technical University of Crete, TUC developers. I performed traffic control data analysis in Matlab, and developed software to serve as a GUI to create and manage the files required by the TUC software. TUC employed advanced automatic control methodologies which led to improvements in journey times as compared to fixed-time settings under saturated traffic conditions, thereby reducing congestion and contributing to the quality of life in European cities.
