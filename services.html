How I can work with you

I specialize in offering contractor services, primarily focusing on Data & Analytics projects for large organizations, where I serve as a temporary member of the implementation team. Additionally, I provide consulting to companies, advising them on how to derive value from analytics and how to effectively organize their analytical assets architecture. I also offer a blend of training and coaching to empower organizations in their analytical journey.

Contracting

I help large organizations in implementing analytical assets to enhance their decision-making processes, working as a member of the development team on fixed-term contracts.
You may review the projects I've participated throughout my career, here.

Consulting

I assist Data & Analytics teams in optimizing the value derived from their analytical projects. I ensure that their solutions align with business decision-making requirements and that the analytical models are designed for scalability and performance.

Training

Through a combination of training and coaching sessions, I demonstrate how tools like Power BI can be effectively utilized. In these short-term sessions, I equip company teams to jump-start their analytical journey, also sharing insights on succesful analytical projects.


Typical Analytical Deliverables:

BI Reports

Automatic reporting apps offer the information you need to drive your business success. Make proactive decisions with accurate metrics, KPIs, key trends & comparative insights.

Data Engineering

Data Warehousing infrastructure, automatic data flows and governance operations to ensure consistent, accurate and up-to-date reporting & self-service analytics.

AI & Advanced Analytics

With BI reports enhanced with AI & advanced analytics, you can generate deeper insights, making it easier to plan smart strategies and improve productivity.


* An analytical deliverable usually offers a combination of the above features.
