How Matt can work with you

I bring value through BI reports implementation & BI models (re)design, robust Data Warehouse & Data Lakehouse development, and AI & advanced analytics enablement, either as a freelancer or as a contractor in extensive corporate projects. I also provide guidance to organizations on how to design, evolve & derive value from their analytical capabilities, work with business stakeholders to elicit requirements, and help guide development teams.


Typical Analytical Deliverables:

BI Reports

Automatic reporting apps offer the information you need to drive your business success. Make proactive decisions with accurate metrics, KPIs, key trends & comparative insights.

AI & Advanced Analytics

With BI reports enhanced with AI & advanced analytics, you can generate deeper insights, making it easier to plan smart strategies and improve productivity.

Data Engineering

Data Warehousing infrastructure, automatic data flows and governance frameworks to enable and improve the quality of self-service reporting & analytics.


* An analytical deliverable usually offers a combination of the above features. A set of BI reports for example, can have embedded AI & advanced analytics, and provide self-service reporting capabilities through the underlying semantic model.
