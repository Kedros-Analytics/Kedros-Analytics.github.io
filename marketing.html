Spend your marketing budget where it actually works

For SMEs, marketing budgets are usually too tight to waste on guesswork. Here's how data, analytics and AI help you see which campaigns convert, which products sell together, and which leads are worth chasing.

Example 1

Campaign performance tracking

A live dashboard pulling leads and conversions by channel and by campaign shows, in one place, which social platforms are actually turning interest into paying customers — not just clicks.

Decision it enables: which channels and campaigns deserve more budget next month, and which to pause.

Example 2

Market basket analysis

Analyzing which products are bought together reveals natural bundles and cross-sell opportunities that aren't obvious from sales totals alone.

Decision it enables: which products to bundle, promote together, or place near each other, online or in-store.

Example 3

Lead scoring and valuation

Scoring leads and opportunities by their likelihood and expected value turns a flat pipeline list into a ranked one, so sales effort goes where it's most likely to pay off.

Decision it enables: which leads your sales team should call first this week.
