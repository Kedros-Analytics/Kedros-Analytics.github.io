AI chatbot (Copilot) demo

A very simple chatbot I've trained using a couple of old book I had on analytics. You can ask questions about Data Mining, R and ways to generate business value from analytics, and it will give answers based on those books (it will also references the books in case you want to buy them). It demonstrates the effectiveness of modern Microsoft AI chatbots (copilots), and is quite easy to build in Copilot Studio. Please note it only works on desktop browsers, the process to embedd it in a mobile app is different.
